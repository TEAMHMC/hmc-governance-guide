About the Opportunity

Health Matters Clinic (HMC) welcomes individuals committed to health equity, community wellness, and collaborative leadership to apply for service on our Board of Directors or Community Advisory Council. This process ensures thoughtful onboarding, orientation, and engagement consistent with HMC’s governance standards.

Orientation Process

  1. Submit your completed application below.
  2. Upon submission, you will receive a confirmation email with orientation details.
  3. Orientation includes viewing recorded sessions and reviewing official governance materials.
  4. Complete the Orientation Acknowledgment form (tracked via Airtable).
  5. Once your orientation is complete, you’ll be invited to the next scheduled Board or CAB meeting via Google Meet.
