Overview

Health Matters Clinic (HMC) is a nonprofit community health organization dedicated to equitable access to care. This guide outlines our governance structure, policies, and application process for those interested in serving on our Board of Directors or Community Advisory Council.

  • Mission: Break down barriers to health for all.
  • Governance: Board of Directors provides fiduciary oversight; Community Advisory Council represents lived experience and community voice.
  • Committees: Executive, Governance & Nominations, Finance & Audit, Programs & Quality, Development, and Advocacy.
